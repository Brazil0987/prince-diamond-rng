How Rarities Work

Every item is in one big roll pool. The game uses each item's 1 in X number to choose what you get.

Weight formula: weight = 1 / chance.

That means a item with 1 in 500 has more weight than a item with 1 in 5000.

Bigger 1 in X = rarer item.

Common
Below 1 in 5
Rare
1 in 5 to 1 in 99
Epic
1 in 100 to 1 in 199
Legendary
1 in 200 to 1 in 499
Mythic
1 in 500 to 1 in 9,999
Secret
1 in 10,000 to 1 in 9,999,999
Diamond Tier
1 in 10,000,000+

Examples

1 in 500: weight = 0.002

1 in 5000: weight = 0.0002

So a 1 in 500 item is about 10x more likely than a 1 in 5000 item.

A Secret item does not need to hit a Secret tier first. It is Secret because its 1 in X number is high.

Important Notes

Hidden items stay secret: this page explains the system without revealing special hidden item names.

Boosts: Luck boosts Mythic, Secret, and Diamond Tier items. Secret Upgrade boosts Secret and Diamond Tier items. Speed only makes rolls faster.
